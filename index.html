Vesak 2025 Volunteer Experience

In May 2025, I had the honor to serve as a volunteer coordinator and welcoming assistant at the international celebration of Vesak 2025. This was a very large Buddhist festival with thousands of participants from many countries. My main responsibility was to support international guests, including pilgrims from India and delegations from Thailand, Sri Lanka, and Myanmar. I helped them during the arrival process, guided them to the ceremony area, and made sure that their time of worship and cultural exchange was smooth and respectful.
